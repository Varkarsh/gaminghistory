Welcome!

Welcome to the world of Video Gaming! This website is here to enrich you nerds with the power (Now you're playing with SUPER POWER!) of the two best things ever: Video Games and History! Now this will be considered a "Crash Course" in terms of: basic knowledge on what happened in said era and what game(s) made an impact. This page is simply a welcome to the website and you can navigate around the rich content here! now if you have any questions simply contact me!

I will begin the journey into the world from the 1950s when a couple of students from Cambridge invented the first video game on their computer. Travelling onwards to the 1970s where Arcades ushered in a new era of digital entertainment. Afterwards, will follow the beginning and temporary end to the console era witrh the video game crash of 1976 and 1982. Which was revived thanks to Nintendo in 1985, and has been popular until the present, and the future to come! I will also be discussing certain games that brought forth a new era (e.g the current era of Battle Royale games like Fortnite)
Every game has contributed in its own way be it positive or negative throughout the community. Eventually the once hobby became an actual subject of prizes with E-Sports and video game competitions.
